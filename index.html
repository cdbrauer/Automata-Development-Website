Services

At Automata Development, we provide a variety of services to help inventors, startups, and makers develop their ideas.

Design

CAD Models

3D scale models that are useful for visualizing your product and sharing it with manufacturers. They also serve as the foundation for creating design drawings, renderings, and prototypes.

Design Drawings

2D scale drawings of your product that can be used for patents or dimensioned and sent to machine shops.

Renderings

Realistic, computer-generated images which can be used on sell sheets and websites to showcase your product

Prototyping

3D Printing

Low-cost plastic components in almost any shape

Applications include creating visual models and prototyping parts that will be injection molded or machined.

Laser Cutting

Flat components cut from wood or plastic

Useful for prototyping thin components, creating engraved front panels, and making assembled enclosures

CNC Routering

Carved wood components

A good alternative to laser cutting when thicker materials or a varying cut depth are required

Our Process

Project Scope

Get in touch with us using the form below to tell us about the field and scope of your project.

We will follow up regarding whether we can accept your project and with more information on our services and approximate pricing and project timeframe.

Detailed Info

Once we have confirmed that we can accept your project, we will ask you for the detailed information we will need to complete you project.

Upon recieving this informaton, we will provide you with a quote and timeframe for completion.

Execution

Upon recieving your approval of our quote, we will begin work on your project. We will keep you updated frequently on our progress.

Delivery

When your project is complete, we will share all created documents and files. Physical prototypes can be mailed or local pickup can be arranged.

Project Confidentiality

All data provided by the client will be kept confidential and will be the property of the client. No information regarding the project will be disclosed to any third party without the prior consent of the client. If requested by the client, we are open to signing a non-disclosure agreement.

At Automata Development we also maintain several of our own product lines. The questions on the initial contact form help us to protect clients' intellectual property by allowing us to determine if there will be any conflict of interest with our products before accepting any new projects.

 

Contact

Any information you share with us will be kept confidential. However, we ask that you do not disclose any proprietary intellectual property until we can verify that there will not be a conflict of interest with our other products or clients.
