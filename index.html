Design

CAD Models

3D scale models that are useful for visualizing your product and sharing it with manufacturers. They also serve as the foundation for creating design drawings, renderings, and prototypes.

Design Drawings

2D scale drawings of your product that can be used for patents or dimensioned and sent to machine shops.

Renderings

Realistic, computer-generated images which can be used on sell sheets and websites to showcase your product

Prototyping

3D Printing

Low-cost plastic components in almost any shape

Applications include creating visual models and prototyping parts that will be injection molded or machined.

Laser Cutting

Flat components cut from wood or plastic

Useful for prototyping thin components, creating engraved front panels, and making assembled enclosures

CNC Routering

Carved wood components

A good alternative to laser cutting when thicker materials or a varying cut depth are required
